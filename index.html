Privacy Policy

Application: SHREE PFMS
Last Updated: June 2026

SHREE PFMS is designed to simplify beneficiary management and Excel-based data processing for authorized users working within the Public Financial Management System (PFMS). Protecting user privacy is our top priority.

1. Information We Collect

SHREE PFMS does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information with the developer or any third party.

2. Local Data Processing

All Excel files, beneficiary records, and user settings are processed locally within the browser. Data remains securely on the user's device and is used solely to provide the extension's intended functionality.

3. Browser Permissions

To function seamlessly entirely on your device, the extension requires the following localized permissions:

  • storage – Used to save user preferences and locally stored extension data.
  • unlimitedStorage – Allows local storage of large Excel datasets without data loss.
  • contextMenus – Provides convenient right-click menu options for extension features.

4. Third-Party Services

SHREE PFMS operates in complete isolation. It does not execute remote code, utilize advertising networks, embed analytics trackers, or collect telemetry datasets from users.

5. Data Sharing

No user information is ever sold, rented, transferred, or disclosed to any third party under any circumstances.

6. Security

Because all operations are contained inside your local browser environment, the security of your data relies on your device. Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their own computer systems and browser profiles.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to align with evolving browser standards. Any revisions will be published instantly on this page along with the updated effective date.

8. Contact

For questions, technical support, or feedback regarding SHREE PFMS, please reach out to the developer using the direct contact information listed on our official extension page.
